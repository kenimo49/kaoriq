## The Problem with How We Choose Fragrance

Walk into any department store fragrance section and you'll encounter the same ritual: a salesperson sprays a strip, you sniff, you nod politely, and you leave overwhelmed. The entire industry is built on two selling mechanisms — celebrity endorsement and seasonal trends.

Neither has anything to do with whether the scent actually suits *you*.

## What Research Says

Studies in psychophysiology have repeatedly shown that scent preferences correlate strongly with personality traits. A 2015 study published in *Flavour and Fragrance Journal* found that individuals scoring high in Openness (one of the Big Five personality traits) consistently gravitated toward complex, layered compositions — woody-oriental blends with unexpected notes like vetiver, incense, and dried fruit.

Meanwhile, those scoring high in Agreeableness preferred cleaner, more transparent compositions — fresh citrus, white florals, light musks.

This isn't coincidence. It's neuroscience.

## The Big Five Connection

The Big Five personality model (OCEAN) maps to fragrance preferences in predictable ways:

| Trait | High Score Preference | Low Score Preference |
|-------|----------------------|---------------------|
| **Openness** | Complex, unusual compositions | Familiar, crowd-pleasing scents |
| **Conscientiousness** | Clean, structured fragrances | Casual, easygoing blends |
| **Extraversion** | Bold, projecting sillage | Intimate, skin-close scents |
| **Agreeableness** | Soft, approachable notes | Sharp, distinctive accords |
| **Neuroticism** | Comforting, warm base notes | Energizing, bright top notes |

## Why This Matters

When you choose a fragrance based on your personality profile rather than what's trending on TikTok, two things happen:

1. **You actually wear it.** Impulse purchases based on hype end up sitting in a drawer. Personality-matched scents become daily signatures.
2. **Others notice.** A fragrance that aligns with your personality feels *coherent* to people around you. It doesn't clash with how you carry yourself.
